Multnomah County Lobbyist Ordinance

Software Requirements & Compliance Matrix

The Multnomah County Government Accountability Ordinance establishes comprehensive lobbyist registration and transparency requirements. This matrix shows how each ordinance requirement translates into software functionality, organized by three implementation tiers: Direct Enforcement (prevention), Detection & Auditing (flagging), and Attestation & Education (accountability).

Effective Date: July 1, 2026 | Ordinance Approved: April 2, 2025

Implementation Tiers

Tier 1: Direct Enforcement (27 items) - Form validation, required fields, deadline tracking
Tier 2: Detection & Auditing (8 items) - Cross-referencing, pattern detection, anomaly flagging
Tier 3: Attestation & Education (11 items) - Required certifications, help text, tracking
