üé® Design Notes & Decisions

Multi-Step Form Approach

Why: Reduces cognitive load, clear progress indicator, allows save/resume, easier validation per section

Alternative: Single-page accordion form (all sections visible, collapse/expand)

Prominent Alert Box

Why: 3-day deadline is critical, links to exemption checker, sets expectations

Hours Spent Field

Why: Validates 10+ hour requirement, helps users self-check eligibility

Alternative: Could link to separate hour-tracking tool

Subject Text Area

Why: Free-form allows flexibility, examples guide users

Alternative: Predefined categories with checkboxes + "other" field

Save Draft Button

Why: Long form, users may need to gather information, prevents data loss

Accessibility Considerations

  • Required fields marked with asterisk and aria-required
  • Help text associated with inputs (aria-describedby)
  • Clear focus indicators (ring-2 on focus)
  • Logical tab order
  • High contrast colors
